Greensboro Legal Access

Connecting Citizens to Social Engineers

The Navigator decodes the law, but these organizations provide the representation. If you are facing a legal crisis in Greensboro, reach out to these verified pro-bono pillars.

Legal Aid of North Carolina

Primary

The statewide nonprofit providing free civil legal help to low-income North Carolinians. Specialized in housing defense, domestic violence, and consumer rights.

Visit legalaidnc.org Call (336) 272-0148

Guilford County Courthouse

Local Govt

The central hub for filing legal responses, staying evictions, and accessing court records. This is where you bring the documents decoded by the B&E Navigator to exercise your rights.

Court Location & Hours Call (336) 412-7800

NC Free Legal Answers

Online Clinic

A virtual legal clinic through the NC Bar Foundation. Low-income residents can submit civil legal questions to be answered by volunteer attorneys.
