1931: The Legal Desert

The Scottsboro Boys

In March 1931, nine African American teenagers were falsely accused and forced into a legal system designed to ignore them. Their trials were a race against the clock, conducted without adequate counsel or a basic understanding of the charges. They didn't just lack a lawyer; they lacked a Navigator to decode the trap they were in.
